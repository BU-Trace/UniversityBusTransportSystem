# Notice System

## What is the Notice System?
The Notice System is used to send important information to all users.

Notices can be:
- Text
- PDF files

---

## How students see notices
Students receive:
- Popup notification
- Notice list inside the notice button

They can:
- Open the notice
- Download PDF if available

---

## How admin creates a notice
1. Go to **Notice Management**
2. Click **Create Notice**
3. Enter title and content or upload PDF
4. Publish the notice

Once published:
- All users receive a notification
- The notice appears in the notice board

---

## Deleting notices
Admins can delete notices to:
- Clear outdated information
- Remove incorrect notices
