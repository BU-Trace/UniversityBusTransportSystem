# Common Problems and Solutions

## I cannot login
- Check email and password
- Make sure your account is approved
- Contact admin if the problem continues

---

## I cannot see any buses
- The bus may not be active
- No route may be assigned
- Check again later

---

## Map is not updating
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Make sure location permission is enabled

---

## I see a permission error
- Some features are role-based
- Students cannot access admin pages
- Drivers cannot edit routes

---

## Still facing issues?
Contact the admin or support team for help.
